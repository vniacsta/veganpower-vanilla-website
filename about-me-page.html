About Me and this Healthy Project

My name is Vânia, I'm 30 years old and I'm vegan since September 2019 - we're never too late to start making better choices 😊

I decided to create the VEGAN POWER project to raise awareness and share my experience. I believe most people don't have enough information to make healthier choices. This blog is to enlighten you. Keep an open mind. Veganism must happen for a sustainable future. And, trust me, you will feel so much better! 💪

We should eat to live and not live to eat.

VEGAN POWER is for anyone who is interested in helping save the planet 🌍, stopping animal cruelty 🐮🐔 and having a better healthier lifestyle 🌱.
